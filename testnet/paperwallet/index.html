Microfi Free Flow Project

Bitcoin Cash CashTokens paper wallet

on testnet (chipnet)

Send min 1600 satoshi (0.000016 tBCH) and only one category of CashTokens to a paper wallet and share the private key of the paper wallet with someone (e.g. as a gift).
The private key of the paper wallet can be swept in the Microfi testnet wallet (https://microfi.eu/testnet/).
