Discover the power of CashTokens with Microfi Free Flow


Summary

Microfi Free Flow (XMI) is a digital asset released on Bitcoin Cash blockchain as a token of appreciation with no profit incentive.

XMI CashTokens can be used to commemorate special events, show appreciation to others, or even express oneself creatively.

Despite the lack of profit incentive, Microfi Free Flow can still be a valuable crypto-asset for those who appreciate its sentimental and collectible value.

Users should be aware that XMI can also be a volatile and speculative crypto-asset.
